Sharpen Your Tools

Make sure you have installed Sass/Compass or CodeKit correctly. This way you are able to discover the goodness we prepared for you.

Once you have everything in place, read the documentation and find out what’s avaliable. We hooked you up with some mixins and some very fine patterns. Discover all of that goodness in our docs.

Work Your Design Magic

The dancefloor is now yours! Start with your settings. Finetune your forms, pimp your buttons, align your images, design your pages, refine the typography and make love to that whitespace.

To show you how different a design can be, we present you the following nice examples: OnTheRocks.be, OpZoekNaarTalent.be and WorldScoutEducationCongress.org.
